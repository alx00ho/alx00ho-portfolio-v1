During my time as an undergraduate student, I ran into a couple of laptop issues, battery and RAM failures, that forced me to learn new development sequences quickly to conitnue to be active in group projects and finishing class assignments on time. One of my favorite projects that was a Rocket League Stats webpage that took the replays of professional Rocket League and complied them into a spreadsheet-like table for users to nitpick and critique their favorite players and teams. Another project that I thoroughly enjoyed was Ancient Path Adventures where my team and I created a web application for their game, allowing teachers to create and assign modules that students would be able to join and interact with.
