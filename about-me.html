I currently work at Tea Hill as a Team Leader, help serve various kinds of Taiwanese teas for customers!

Growing up, I have always been captivated by creating projects with my hands. Puzzles, K'nex building sets, and the like caught my attention. I would spend hours working patiently with a detailed eye to placing the pieces together. Puzzles are still one of my favorite pastimes, where I can listen to enticing albums and podcasts while at work.

I discovered the Software Developer career path in high school, which fascinated both the logical and creative parts of my brain and thus pursued and achieved a Bachelor of Science C.S. degree from the University of North Carolina in 2022. During my undergraduate career, I learned that time is very precious and to use it wisely between my peers, family, friends, and myself. Quickly learning my development skills made me an active contributor to group projects and finish class assignments on time.

One of my favorite projects is a Rocket League Statistics web application that leveraged the ballchasing API to compile data from both professional and collegiate Rocket League matches into a dynamic, searchable table for users to search for their favorite players and teams and compare various statistics. We added user authentication and the option for them to favorite players and teams and pool them into a personalized list. Players and teams also had pop-up info cards that held their nationality and team history.

Another project that I enjoyed was a web application for Promised Land Journey, a game developed by Ancient Path Adventures (APA). We developed a platform where APA could create modules stored in a database. These modules consisted of quizzes, videos, and readings. Teachers were allowed to use these example modules or make their own after paying a subscription to use APA's services. Upon class creation, teachers could give out their unique, randomly generated access code for students to join their classes and take the modules.

Outside of my technical skills, I enjoy dabbling in other creative avenues. Being a former percussionist, I try to maintain and grow my playing skills by performing with the nearby church or some rudimental drumming. I've also been developing my listening and composition skills on the side. Cooking helps me connect with my Vietnamese heritage and is something I want to share with my friends and future family. The newest of my hobbies is 3D rendering, where I've followed the Blender Guru's tutorial on a donut animation and plan on bringing places I've been into the digital world.
